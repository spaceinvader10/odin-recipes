Lemon Chicken Pasta

  1. 1 (16 ounce) package linguine pasta
  2. ½ cup diced onion
  3. 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch chunks
  4. kosher salt and ground black pepper to taste
  5. ½ (8 ounce) package reduced-fat cream cheese, cubed and softened
  6. ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  7. 2 teaspoons lemon zest
  8. ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  9. ¼ cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  10. ¼ cup chopped flat-leaf (Italian) parsley
  11. 1 cup frozen peas
  12. 2 tablespoons butter
  13. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  14. 1 (8 ounce) package button mushrooms, sliced
